The Challenge

The Sharpest Double-Edged SwordTM

AI is the sharpest double-edged sword humanity has ever forged. It can, if we work together, create a golden age of humanity dreamed of only by the most idealistic among us. However, in the wrong hands, AI can bring about the end of human history in terrifying fashion that would make even the most dystopian writers shudder.

Inevitably as it develops, AI will cause unprecedented unemployment levels in the hundreds of millions and likely entirely end the need for a human workforce. If we allow an elite, self-interested few to control AI's present and future, AI will bring about the American nightmare and destroy humanity.

We must stop them. AI development needs to be a product of all of our collective guidance and done for all of our benefit. The story of AI should be one of unlocking human potential and thriving at a scale we've never seen, rather than one where techno-elitists destroy the human experiment.
