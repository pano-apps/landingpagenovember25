The Challenge

AI is a double-edged sword

We're in the middle of a battle for the present and future of humanity. AI will create such a rapid pace of innovation and job displacement that we can't afford to let its development be guided solely by profit motives.

This isn't doomerism or a Luddite philosophy. It's a recognition that most of the wealthy elites developing AI have a "move fast and break things" philosophy that prioritizes their enrichment over what's good for the average person.

That needs to change. AI development needs to be executed thoughtfully, in a way that benefits all people‚Äînot just a fortunate few.
