A Message from Our Founder

Our Story and Mission

B

Brian Patrick

Founder, Panodime

From a young age, the world always struck me as fundamentally unfair given that so many people lack equal opportunity, despite the abundance of the modern era. I studied Political Theory at Georgetown and went to law school at Harvard so I could dedicate my life working to make the American Dream a reality for everyone everywhere.

What the American Dream Means to Me

To me, the American Dream is more than just the ability to rise from nothing to economic success, but it's the ability to be fully educated and informed so you can decide for yourself, the ability to pursue a career that you find meaningful, and the ability to be able to spend time with those you love.

We Have an Opportunity no Humans Have Ever Had

After studying history, law and politics for many years, it struck me that we live in the most unique and important time in human history. Technological advances combined with widespread collective action have the potential to make the American Dream a reality for everyone and bring about the world humans have always deserved. Yet technology has not lead to fairness, justice or equality. It has been used be the rich and powerful to control, manipulate, and oppresss most people. And no technology embodies this potential for helping humanity, and the reality that elites are using it to oppress humanity, more than AI.

The Two Paths Forward: One is a Dream

However, AI is the sharpest double-edged sword we've ever forged. If we wreslte control from the elites over that technology, so that all people may alter the present and future of AI to ensure their rights are respected, together we can use AI as part of a strategy to boldly carve out a new world. A world that has only existed in the minds of idealists. A world where everyone can avoid mundane drudgery, finding fulfillment with the ones they love and purpose in the passions they cultivate.

The Other is a Nightmare

But, if we allow an elite, self-interested few to control AI's present and future, AI will bring about the American nightmare, and become destructive and an existential threat to humanity. In their hands alone, our future is at best suffering under an AI techno-elitist regime where the few with power over AI conspire with the technology to rule in their own interest, and where at worst the technology itself becomes its own species that eradicates humanity entirely.

My Commitment to Realizing the Dream

I am here because I am dedicating the rest of my life to ensuring we collectively harness AI to bring about that fair and peaceful world. The world where everyone can pursue their own meaningful contributions to humanity and have time to share their lives with the ones they love. This is not romanticism or idealism to think we can do this. It's a product of the stark reality that we have developed the most powerful technology in human history that can create humanity's most inspiring dreams or fuel our darkest nightmares.
